Email setup

I started using a custom domain in early 2020, and haven't looked back. It's not only fun, and aesthetically pleasing, but it gives me more control over the way I engage with email providers and online services. I chose Fastmail for hosting since I felt as though it struck a nice balance between having modern email-client functionality, and not being a massive tech firm. Unfortunately Fastmail still falls under the Five Eyes surveillance alliance, but it's a tradeoff.
